History Of Dobu Martial Arts

DoBu Martial Arts had humble beginnings in London, United Kingdom back in 2005. It all started with a passionate martial artist and coach, Mauricio Gomez, who envisioned creating a space where individuals could discover their true potential through martial arts.

In the early years, our gym operated in a small studio in the vibrant neighborhood of Soho, offering training in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai. The dedication of our instructors and the commitment of our first members laid the foundation for what DoBu Martial Arts would become.

As the years passed, our vision expanded. We moved to our current spacious location, which boasts top-notch training facilities. We diversified our offerings to include various martial arts disciplines, fitness training, and specialist courses, catering to a wider range of interests and skill levels.

Today, DoBu Martial Arts stands as a symbol of dedication, empowerment, and growth. We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment where individuals of all backgrounds and aspirations can thrive. Our future is as bright as our past, and we're excited to continue supporting our members on their journeys to greatness.

Mission

At DoBu Martial Arts, our mission is to empower individuals through martial arts, fitness, and personal growth. We are dedicated to providing a welcoming and inclusive community where people of all backgrounds can discover their inner strength, enhance their physical fitness, and build lifelong skills. Through world-class coaching and a commitment to excellence, we strive to instill confidence, discipline, and respect in every member, fostering a culture of determination and self-improvement.

Vision

Our vision is to be the leading martial arts center, recognized for transforming lives through martial arts and fitness. We aspire to continue expanding our diverse offerings, providing cutting-edge training facilities, and maintaining a team of passionate and experienced instructors. We aim to inspire individuals to pursue their goals, both inside and outside the gym, as they become champions in their own lives. DoBu Martial Arts envisions a world where people harness their inner potential to lead healthier, more empowered, and fulfilling lives.
